# 🎬 LIVE DEMO SCRIPT: MULTI-MIND SIMULATOR

This script is designed for a **10-minute technical demonstration**. It highlights the agentic architecture, high-fidelity UI, and the industrial research output of the **Multi-Mind Simulator v12.5**.

---

## 🕒 [00:00 - 01:30] PHASE 1: THE OPENING HOOK
**Action**: Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000). Ensure the "Neural HUD" is visible.

**Script**:
> "Welcome to the Multi-Mind Simulator—v12.5, the Industrial Research Edition. We aren't just looking at another chatbot; we are looking at an **Adversarial Agentic Workflow** designed for high-stakes scientific and ethical deliberation.
>
> Notice the **Forensic HUD** and the reactive particles. This is the **Neural Sync Dashboard**, where complex global topics—from Bio-Ethics in CRISPR to the Geo-Politics of AGI—are analyzed by four distinct machine minds."

**Point out**:
*   The **Topic Cards**: Rapidly hover over "Medical Ethics" or "Geo-Politics."
*   **The Neural Council Manifest**: Scroll down to show Oopsinator, Professor Doom, Sarcastron, and Glitchy.
*   **Key Phrase**: "Every agent has a unique persona-modulated logic core, strictly de-coupled for adversarial integrity."

## 🕒 [01:30 - 04:00] PHASE 2: AGENTIC WORKFLOW & REASONING
**Action**: Enter a complex topic (e.g., *"Should AI be granted personhood in the court of law?"*) and click **INITIALIZE ARENA**.

**Script**:
> "As the simulation initializes, our **Strategic Planner AI** is extracting the semantic core of this prompt. It decides the turn-taking sequence to maximize logical conflict.
>
> Look at the **Thought Panel** for Oopsinator. This isn't a direct response—it’s a **Chain-of-Thought (CoT)** reasoning pass. The agent is simulating its own logical path *before* committing to speech. This fulfills the **Agent Design** requirement: Input → Reasoning → Action."

**Point out**:
*   The **Pulsing Background**: It turns **Cyan** for Oopsinator and **Red** for Professor Doom.
*   The **Reasoning Blocks**: Read a line from a "Thought" bubble to show depth.
*   **Neural Verification**: Point at the `NEURAL_RETRIEVAL_TOOL...` signal.

---

## 🕒 [04:00 - 06:00] PHASE 3: VERIFICATION & TRANSPARENCY
**Action**: Hover over a **Fact-Check Badge** and **CLICK** the source link.

**Script**:
> "Transparency is critical. Every statement made by the Council is processed through our **Neural Retrieval Tool**. See this 'STRONG' verdict? The AI identified a claim, ran a verification query, and retrieved a citation.
>
> Unlike typical LLMs, we provide **clickable source links**. If I click this link to NASA or the WHO, I can verify the ground truth immediately. This eliminates the 'hallucination' barrier in industrial AI."

## 🕒 [06:00 - 08:30] PHASE 4: HUMAN INTERACTION (THE WOW MOMENT)

**Script**:
